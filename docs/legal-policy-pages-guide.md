# Legal Policy Pages Guide

OutcomeIQ includes public MVP policy pages so evaluators, deployment reviewers and payment-provider reviewers can see the expected public routes.

## Public routes

- `/privacy`
- `/terms`
- `/refund-policy`
- `/contact`

## Current status

The pages are practical MVP placeholders. They explain:

- OutcomeIQ is currently a demo/MVP.
- Real payments are not enabled.
- The app uses simulated AI provider data.
- Sensitive real customer data should not be entered into the MVP.
- Formal legal review is required before commercial launch.

## Production requirement

Before launch, replace placeholder copy with lawyer-reviewed documents covering:

- Data collection and retention.
- User rights and deletion requests.
- Payment and cancellation terms.
- Refund timelines.
- Support obligations.
- Jurisdiction and dispute handling.
- Contact details and business identity.

Do not treat the current pages as final legal advice.
